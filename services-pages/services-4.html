We have deep experience in information security in the highly-regulated government market, achieving one of the first Authorizations to Operate (ATO) for the Department of Health and Human Services using the revised Security Assessment and Authorization (SA&A) process, under FISMA. Our work has been used as an example within Federal Agencies.
