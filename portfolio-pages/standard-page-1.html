BioSense Platform

InductiveHealth supports the BioSense Platform for the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), working alongside partners ICF International and Deloitte Consulting. The BioSense Platform and the National Syndromic Surveillance Program it is a part of are the primary tools used by the CDC for nationwide syndromic surveillance. The platform currently receives data on over 60% of emergency department visits nationwide as well as other data sources, and contains data spanning over a decade.

InductiveHealth is responsible for all external integrations into the BioSense Platform and supports data warehousing, data analytics, systems engineering, and maintenance and operations of the system. As a result of our work, the program now has access to important new datasets and has implemented several new platform features that increase data quality and have dramatically improved the future scalability of the system.
