InductiveHealth is the sole license holder of ESSENCE (Electronic Surveillance System for the Early Notification of Community-based Epidemics), developed by the Johns Hopkins University Applied Physics Laboratory (APL) and utilized by more than 25 jurisdictions at the state, regional, and federal levels including the National Syndromic Surveillance Program at CDC. This important partnership enables InductiveHealth to host ESSENCE as a SaaS offering including top-tier support to jurisdictions and allows APL to focus on research and development. ESSENCE is a disease surveillance system that integrates numerous health-related data sources, including emergency department, mortality, poison, weather data, and more. The system facilitates real-time monitoring and alerting for public health officials and epidemiologists through unique algorithms that detect public health threats and events including disease outbreaks. InductiveHealth’s partnership with APL means that we’re at the cutting edge of syndromic and other integrated disease surveillance technology. Read the press release.
