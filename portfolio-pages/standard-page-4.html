Genomic Computing Platform

Whole genome sequencing (WGS) and other advanced molecular testing methods have put new strains on laboratories. InductiveHealth developed the Inductive Genomic Computing Platform to provide an integrated suite of WGS analysis tools, a scalable computing and storage environment, and bioinformatics support into one simple offering. No longer do lab directors need to figure out servers and storage arrays or find ways to configure numerous complex toolsets to make effective use of this data for public health analysis.

Our technology integrates directly with leading lab testing equipment to simplify workflows and get to the analysis results faster, increasing lab productivity and testing throughput. We designed for scalability at the start, allowing us to offer this service at a cost significantly below what it would take to build similar capabilities in-house.
