HIV Drug Resistance DW

Monitoring the emergence of drug resistance in HIV is of critical importance to managing the long-term effectiveness of existing ARV treatments and to guide the deployment of second-line drugs. InductiveHealth was asked by CDC to develop a new approach for analyzing HIV drug resistance globally, building the HIV Drug Resistance Data Warehouse.

This system combines whole genome sequence data for HIV samples and combines it with epidemiolgic data in a unified platform for simple data analysis. The system then pulls data from external reference sources and analyzes the collected HIV genetic sequence data for markers that suggest the emergence of drug resistance in a given strain. As a result of the successful design and implementation of the HIV Drug Resistance Data Warehouse, researchers now have a powerful new tool to monitor and manage the HIV crisis globally.
