InductiveHealth Respond™

To combat the coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic, many jurisdictions identified the need to scale contact tracing capabilities quickly and in alignment with policy and guidance from state and federal agencies. InductiveHealth created Respond™ for Contact Tracing and Symptom Monitoring. Our Respond Plus™ offering includes integration with data from other systems such as electronic disease surveillance systems (EDSS), travel portals, and more. Multiple jurisdictions are actively using Respond™ to notify and monitor those who have a possible or known exposure to the virus. While COVID-19 is top of mind, it can be leveraged for other conditions including STIs. Our solution is flexible to meet your jurisdiction’s needs.
